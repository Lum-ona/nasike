Westo Recyclers – No Waste

Sustainability. Circular Economy. Green Jobs

This is an Afosi an ecopreneurship based in the informal settlements in Nairobi. The initiative seeks to promote sustainability and inclusivity – for the environment, communities, and people by creating a clean and safe environment for our generation.

The objectives of the initiatives are to:

  1. Promote a “green” environment and sustainable growth in waste collection
  2. Map and enumerate stakeholders in the informal waste-supply chain in the informal settlements, cities and towns in Kenya and create an end-to-end recyclable waste logistic platform
  3. Deliver quality materials to recyclers and valuable recycled products to the industries
  4. Create jobs among the youth in the informal settlements
