Environment

Environment

All countries depend heavily on their environment: Forests, grasslands, rivers, lakes, coral reefs and other ecosystems provide essential resources that provide food, water, shelter and energy. But these ecosystems and resources are under incredible pressure, hence the need to put in place mitigation adaptation and resilience building mechanisms

Our Outcomes are

  • Improved forest cover in the communities
  • Adoption of ESD and STEM4SD practices in learning institutions
  • Informed and responsible citizenry on environmental sustainability

ENVIRONMENTAL SUSTAINABILITY

Fighting deforestation and planting trees, so that forests continue to provide clean water and air, improve agricultural productivity, slow the rate of climate change, counter desertification, and support economic growth; Mitigating and adapting to the effects of climate change, so communities can grow without harming the environment while strengthening their resilience to warmer temperatures and weather shocks.

What we do
  • Greening Kenya Campaign
  • Education for Sustainable Development in learning institutions
  • STEM for Sustainable Development (STEM4SD)
  • Promoting Green businesses
  • Developing the resilience community of practice

CLIMATE JUSTICE

Climate change has a polarizing effect, aggravating inequalities and reducing access to justice for those most at risk.As Afosi, we work with communities to better manage and benefit from their natural resources.We gather and share stories, highlighting the voices from the frontlines, centering climate impacts and people, to push governments to urgently respond to the climate emergency.

  • Citizen engagement
