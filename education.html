Education

Education

Education serves as a driver for development and the elimination of extreme poverty. Education is transformational for individuals and societies. It creates pathways to better health, economic growth, a sustainable environment, and peaceful, democratic societies. A person’s earnings increase by 10 percent with each year of school they complete. Women with higher levels of education have healthier children. And increasing the average level of higher education in a country by just one year can add half a percentage point of growth to GDP. The target for our education program young people in secondary schools, TVETs and youth out of school.

Our outcomes are:
  • Increased uptake of emerging technologies for improved learning
  • Increased products that are commercialized among the learners

Promote inclusion and uptake of emerging technologies in education

  • Mentorship
  • STEM Education
  • Digital Literacy
  • 21st century skills

Promote the commercialization of student’s innovations

  • Boot camps
  • Mentorship and Linkages with partners
