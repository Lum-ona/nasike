OSYSTEI

Out-of-School Youth Science, Technology and Innovation (OSYSTEI) Program initiative seeks to catalyse pro-youth actions to address the dreams, ambitions and hopes of the out of school youth in the country by creating a culture of curiosity, creativity, innovation and productive entrepreneurship. The program actualises the endorsement of H.E the President of the Republic of Kenya as the Global Youth Champion on youth education, training and employment.


The OSYSTEI program is focused on the application of science, technology and innovation in creating alternative and productive enterprises that will lead to job and wealth creation.

The overall aspiration is to:

Introduce a national science, technology and innovation exhibition for out of school youth II. Deliver opportunities and services to help young people acquire scientific, technological and innovative skills using their existing knowledge and experience III. Offer occupational skills training to enable youth become innovators and job creators in areas that will support the Big Four Agenda-healthcare, manufacturing, food security and housing IV. Focus on skills acquisition for enhancing entrepreneurship, creativity and innovations driven by faithful adherence to our national values and ethics.
